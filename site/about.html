💎 About DirectSponsor

🌍 Revolutionary peer-to-peer charity platform

DirectSponsor eliminates the charity industrial complex by connecting sponsors directly with verified recipients. No organizations. No administrative fees. No power abuse.

We believe charity should be peer-to-peer, transparent, and direct. Every dollar you send goes straight to the person doing the work.

🚨 The Charity Problem We're Solving

Traditional charity organizations have become part of the problem:

Issue Impact Example
Administrative overhead 20-80% of donations Red Cross: 91¢ of every dollar goes to "programs"
Executive salaries Millions per year United Way CEO: $1.2M+ annually
Lack of transparency Donors never know Where did YOUR $50 actually go?
Power concentration Organizations control everything Recipients have no direct voice

💡 Sources: Charity Navigator, GuideStar, annual reports

✨ Our Revolutionary Approach

🔍 Web of Trust

Every recipient verified through academic institutions, site visits, and multi-year relationships

👤 Individual Focus

Sponsor people, not organizations. Direct accountability and personal relationships

₿ Direct Payments

Bitcoin/Lightning Network ensures 100% of funds reach recipients instantly

📱 Real Updates

Photos, progress reports, and direct communication from the people you sponsor

🎯 Our Core Values

  • 🔒 Transparency First - Every transaction recorded in public accounts
  • 🎓 Academic Rigor - University-level verification standards
  • 👥 Human Connection - Personal relationships over institutional bureaucracy
  • ⚡ Direct Impact - No intermediaries, no delays, no fees
  • 🌱 Quality over Quantity - Carefully verified projects only
  • 💰 Economic Empowerment - Sustainable income, not handouts

🎯 How DirectSponsor Works

Traditional charities keep 20-90% of donations for "overhead." With DirectSponsor you send 100% of your money directly to the people you support:

1️⃣ Verification

Every project verified through our Web of Trust network

2️⃣ Direct Payment

Bitcoin/Lightning payments straight to recipients

3️⃣ Real Updates

Direct communication and photo updates from your recipient

4️⃣ Measurable Impact

See exactly what your money accomplished

🔐 How We Verify Projects

Our Web of Trust verification requires multiple independent sources:

📚 Academic Verification

University professors, researchers, and academic institutions provide credentials and ongoing oversight.

Example: Evans verified by University of Nairobi professor

✈️ In-Person Site Visits

Trusted professionals visit projects in person and provide detailed reports.

Example: Professional fundraiser from Ireland visited Kenya

📅 Multi-Year Relationships

We build long-term personal relationships and track progress over years.

Example: 3+ year relationship with Evans and team

🎥 Documentary Evidence

Video documentation, YouTube channels, and continuous progress updates.

Example: Grant & Annegret's "Green and Abundant Farming" channel
