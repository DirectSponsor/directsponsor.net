🌱 Submit a Project for Verification

Want to add a project to DirectSponsor? Our Web of Trust verification requires multiple independent sources. Here's what we need:

📚 Academic Connection

  • University professor or researcher who can verify the project
  • Academic institution involvement or oversight
  • Research component or educational value

✈️ In-Person Verification

  • Trusted professional who has visited the project
  • Local community leader or government official
  • Independent verification from multiple sources

📅 Track Record

  • Minimum 1-year project history
  • Documented progress and results
  • References from community members

🎥 Documentation

  • Photos, videos, or online presence
  • Social media accounts or website
  • Ongoing documentation of work

💡 Quality over quantity - we carefully verify each project through multiple independent sources before adding to DirectSponsor.

⚙️ Technical Information

DirectSponsor is built on open-source principles with accessibility and performance in mind:

  • 🌐 Website: HTML-first with DS-CMS (zero dependencies)
  • ₿ Payments: Bitcoin and Lightning Network
  • 📱 Mobile: Works on any device, including feature phones
  • 🔓 Open Source: Code available on GitHub
  • ⚡ Performance: Fast loading, minimal bandwidth usage
  • ♿ Accessibility: Screen reader friendly, high contrast mode

Technical issues? Found a bug? Want to contribute code? Visit our GitHub repository.

❓ Frequently Asked Questions

How do I sponsor someone?

Browse our verified projects, choose a person to sponsor, and send Bitcoin directly to their wallet. No intermediaries!

How do you verify projects?

Our Web of Trust requires academic verification, in-person visits, multi-year relationships, and documented evidence. See our About page for details.

Do you charge fees?

No. 100% of your payment goes directly to the recipient. We don't take any administrative fees or commissions.

What about tax receipts?

DirectSponsor is not a registered charity organization - we're a peer-to-peer platform. Consult your accountant about tax implications.

Why Bitcoin instead of traditional payments?

Bitcoin enables instant global payments with low fees, no intermediaries, and complete transparency. Perfect for peer-to-peer charity.

Can I cancel my sponsorship?

Yes! Since you control the payments directly, you can stop or modify sponsorship at any time. We recommend giving recipients advance notice when possible.
