â“ Frequently Asked Questions

How do I sponsor someone?

Browse our verified projects, choose a person to sponsor, and send Bitcoin directly to their wallet. No intermediaries!

How do you verify projects?

Our Web of Trust requires academic verification, in-person visits, multi-year relationships, and documented evidence. See our About page for details.

Do you charge fees?

No. 100% of your payment goes directly to the recipient. We don't take any administrative fees or commissions.

What about tax receipts?

DirectSponsor is not a registered charity organization - we're a peer-to-peer platform. Consult your accountant about tax implications.

Why Bitcoin instead of traditional payments?

Bitcoin enables instant global payments with low fees, no intermediaries, and complete transparency. Perfect for peer-to-peer charity.

Can I cancel my sponsorship?

Yes! Since you control the payments directly, you can stop or modify sponsorship at any time. We recommend giving recipients advance notice when possible.
