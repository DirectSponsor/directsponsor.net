🎯 Mission & Impact

Combat droughts and food shortages through strategic reforestation and sustainable agriculture on Rusinga Island.

The project has already planted 200,000 seedlings and aims to plant another 200,000 trees at a rate of 50,000 per year. This food forest will:

  • 🌧️ Mitigate droughts by holding water in the landscape and encouraging rainfall
  • 🌱 Prevent soil erosion through ground cover and root systems
  • 🍎 Provide food security through fruit trees and herbs
  • 🔄 Achieve self-sustainability within 4 years
  • 🌍 Climate impact through carbon sequestration

📖 Learn more: badilisha.org

👨‍🌾 Meet Evans - Project Coordinator

Evans is the heart of the Badilisha Food Forest project. His responsibilities include:

  • 🎓 Technical guidance - Permaculture design and implementation
  • 🏫 Training - Teaching sustainable agriculture to local farmers
  • 🤝 Community networking - Building relationships with stakeholders
  • 📊 Project management - Coordinating all activities and reporting progress
  • 🌱 Direct planting - Hands-on tree planting and maintenance
