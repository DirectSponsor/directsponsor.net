🌵 Grant & Annegret's Desert Farm

📍 Namib Desert, Namibia

✅ Verified Project • Video Documentation
Self-funded research project • Accepting sponsorship

🎯 Mission: Making the Desert Bloom

Grant and Annegret are pioneering desert agriculture in one of the world's harshest environments - the Namib Desert. Their work focuses on:

  • 💧 Water harvesting systems - Capturing and storing precious desert rainfall
  • 🌿 Desert-adapted crops - Developing sustainable food production techniques
  • 🔬 Research documentation - Creating replicable methods for other desert regions
  • 📚 Education & training - Teaching permaculture principles in arid climates
  • 🌍 Climate adaptation - Developing resilience for expanding desert regions

🎥 Watch their work: Green and Abundant Farming YouTube Channel

🌍 Why Desert Agriculture Matters

As climate change expands desert regions worldwide, Grant and Annegret's research becomes increasingly vital:

  • 🏜️ Expanding deserts - Climate change is creating more arid regions globally
  • 🍎 Food security - Need sustainable agriculture for harsh environments
  • 💧 Water scarcity - Techniques for farming with minimal water resources
  • 🌱 Replicable methods - Research can be applied in similar environments worldwide
  • 🔬 Innovation - Pushing boundaries of what's possible in extreme conditions

By sponsoring Grant and Annegret, you're supporting research that could help millions of people in expanding desert regions worldwide.
