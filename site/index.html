Traditional charity organizations suffer from:

  • 🏢 Administrative overhead - up to 80% of donations

  • ❌ Lack of transparency - where does your money really go?
  • 📊 Power abuse - organizations control everything
  • ⏰ Slow impact - bureaucracy delays real help
