Sponsorship Groups

A sponsorship group is a small circle of people making a regular monthly commitment to support one recipient. Unlike a fundraiser, which has a goal and an endpoint, sponsorship is ongoing — providing a recipient with a reliable income and real human relationships.

Payments go directly from sponsor to recipient via Bitcoin Lightning. No intermediary holds funds.

No sponsorship groups are active yet. Recipients can set up their group from their profile page.

How it works

  • Active sponsors make a monthly Lightning payment directly to the recipient. Up to 12 per group.
  • Standby sponsors are volunteers who fill in if an active sponsor misses a month.
  • Queued sponsors are on the waiting list — promoted when a place opens.

A waiting list is a sign of value. Groups are kept small so the relationship stays real.
